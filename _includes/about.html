About Me

I’ve worked solo and in teams. I've dealt with deadlines, disasters, and the strange silence of code that should work but doesn’t. I know what it's like to carry a project when no one else will. I know how to build momentum from zero. And I know how to ship.
I'm not here to follow a script — I’m here to solve problems. To learn fast. To adapt faster. To build things that matter.

I don’t have all the answers. But I know how to find them.
